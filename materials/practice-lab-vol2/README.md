# Practice Lab — BCE AI Catalyst Vol-2, Session 2

Workshop materials for the 90-min practice lab. Source-of-truth files live in this directory; deployable copies are pushed to a Google Drive folder and a pre-built NotebookLM workbook (links below, filled at deployment).

## What's here

- `skills/` — the two **provided** Skills attendees upload to their Claude Projects:
  - `root-cause-investigator.md` — Day 1 (Section Head Analyst)
  - `ops-comms-drafter.md` — Day 2 (Team Leader Booster)
  - `examples/` — example skeletons facilitators use to coach Stage 4 skill-creator authoring (Day 1 + Day 2 parallels)
- `starter-pack/` — the 7-file starter pack each team receives:
  - `01-monthly-ops-workbook/` — 5-tab CSV workbook + internal authoring guide (deviation-story.md)
  - `02-process-manual.md` · `03-incident-brief.md` · `04-vendor-notice.md` · `05-report-template.md` · `06-slide-template.md`
  - `07-site-snapshots/` — labeled images (sourced at deployment from Wikimedia/Unsplash)
- `cards/` — 10 prompt / seed-question / coaching cards (D1–D10)
- `runbook/` — facilitator runbook + pre-flight checklist + submission form spec
